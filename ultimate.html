Ultimate is awesome.

I had never played frisbee before my junior year at Luther when I joined the women's frisbee
team at Luther called Freya. I look forward to practice every day because I've come to
really love the sport.

These are some of the few reasons why ultimate is so great:

  • Inclusiveness. Anyone can play, everyone is accepted, and you don't have to be an exceptional athlete to
    learn how to play the game.
  • Sportsmanship. There are no referees. Players call their own fouls, and if there's a disagreement, it is
    worked out between only the players directly involved. Fairness and honesty is highly encouraged.
  • The people. I have never met a frisbee player that I don't like. Everyone is down to earth, welcoming,
    and friendly.

Some people see frisbee as just a bunch of bros throwing around a disc, but it's much more than that.
The sport has its very own culture which is welcoming and relaxed, but also competitive. It makes for an
atmosphere that no other sport could create.
