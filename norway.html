Semester in Tromso, Norway.

My experience was too great to explain in words, so I have some pictures to do the talking.
I met new friends from so many different places, which taught me a lot about both myself and how I
relate to the world around me.

One of our favorite things to do in the mountains of Norway was:

HIKING!!!!

This is one of the many lakes found in-between the mountains in northern Norway. It's Spring, so the river on the right side was in full force,
but not all the snow had melted, even by June. Believe it or not, this picture was taken around 10:00pm! In the summertime, the sun never sets on Tromso.

This picture shows a big group of us that went hiking one snowy day in March. We have many countries represented here,
but the Germans really take the cake in this photo!

Over one of the last weekends that I was there, I took a week-long trip with some friends and drove to Lofoten,
which is a chain of islands, mountains, and fjords of the Eastern coast of Norway.
